Goldmine
goldmine is a seller support application

Problem:

When IBM sellers are building contact lists on Linkedin Sales Navigator (LISN) there are multiple filters and options but have no way to know what filters give them a targeted list for their product without experience.

Solution:

Goldmine aids sellers by providing tested filters made by experts in their brands to create a focused list of clients who are most likely to be interested in the specific product. it then helps further by increases your connection rate in that list by using proven message frameworks for different scenarios including first touch, follow-up and more.
Tech Stack:
Angular.js, Node.js, Nest.js, MongoDB
